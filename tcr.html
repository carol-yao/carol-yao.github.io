Our goal for this project was to improve the workflow processes for the internal volunteers and employees at Toronto Cat Rescue with a primary focus on the intake process.

RESEARCH

Understanding the bigger picture :emoji:

When I first joined this team, the main goal was to start by fixing the cat intake process because there was a lot of manual data entry by volunteers.

After initial discussions, I proposed we step back and understand the whole process before diving into solutioning.

Miguel and I conducted interviews with 7?8? Of the volunteers and 2 full time employees of the organization to understand each step of the process.

The Stages

Service Design Blueprint :emoji:

From my last internship, I learned about service design blueprints so I proposed we use one here.

It ended up being useful to get everyone working on the project on the same page, as well as help each volunteer understand the other roles since everyone was siloed off.
